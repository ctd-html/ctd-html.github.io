Jason Kunesh

Jason Kunesh, who has been employed by organizations including the Chicago Field Museum, Microsoft, Groupon, IBM, and more, is a design professional who in his own words can generally "be found at the intersection of people and technology.

In coming to speak to us, Jason emphasized the importance of building technology with the user in mind. As Director of User Experience of Obama for America, Jason described the though process that went into planning technology in a way that optimized the user experience. As students, this advice in particular is invaluable as we naturally try to create elegant products but sometimes fail to remember the importance of the user.

Chris Gansen

Chris Gansen is a Software Engineer and IT Specialist who has worked at IBM, on the Obama for America campaign, and most recently is owner of Make No Little Plans, LLC.

Having generously volunteered his time to share experience and knowledge which he has accumulated over several years in the field, Chris helped us understand how his interaction with computers transformed from writing simple calculator programs during math class to designing, building, and shipping high quality software.
